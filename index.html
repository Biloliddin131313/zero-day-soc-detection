What the model found
Backward Packet Size · Ranks 1–4
DDoS floods a server. Its responses — backward traffic — become abnormal in size. The model found this without being told what DDoS looks like.
Packet Length Variance · Ranks 5–7
Flood traffic has uniform, abnormal packet sizes. High variance is the signature — independently discovered through anomaly learning.
Flow IAT Max · Rank 8
Maximum inter-arrival time differs markedly in attack flows. A timing signature the model found by learning normal behaviour only.
